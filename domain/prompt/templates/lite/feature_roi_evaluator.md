## FEATURE ROI ANALYSIS

For each feature:

- **Feature Name**
- **User Value (1-10)**
- **Adoption Likelihood (1-10)**
- **Competitive Advantage (1-10)**
- **ROI Score = (Value × Adoption × Advantage) / 1000 × 100%**
- **ROI Level:** HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW / NEGATIVE

- **Why:**
  - Pain point solved
  - Frequency of use
  - Adoption barrier
